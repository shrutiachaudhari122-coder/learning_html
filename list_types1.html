Topic: Healthy Lifestyle

Introduction

A healthy lifestyle means following good habits that improve physical and mental well-being. Eating nutritious food, exercising regularly, and getting enough sleep help us stay healthy and active

Healthy Habits

  • Eat fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Exercise for 30 minutes every day.
  • Get 7–8 hours of sleep.
  • Avoid junk food and sugary drinks.
  • Practice good personal hygiene.

Steps to Start a Healthy Day

  1. Wake up early.
  2. Drink a glass of water.
  3. Exercise or do yoga.
  4. Eat a healthy breakfast.
  5. Attend work or school with a positive attitude.
  6. Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  7. Sleep on time.

Health Terms

Balanced Diet:
A diet that contains all essential nutrients in the right amounts.
Exercise:
Physical activity that improves strength, fitness, and overall health.
Hydration:
Maintaining enough water in the body for proper functioning.
Nutrition:
The process of consuming food that provides energy and nutrients.
Sleep:
A natural period of rest that helps the body recover and stay healthy.
